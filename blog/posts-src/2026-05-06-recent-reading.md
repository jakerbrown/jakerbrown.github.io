**Saga, Vols 1-12 by Brian Vaughan (author) and Fiona Staples (illustrator)**

Reading this after just welcoming a new child is interesting. It captures the wonder of being a kid and juxtaposes it against a rich space opera. But it is also chock full of vivid alien sex. So I oscillate between thinking "I can't wait to share this with Nathan" before turning the page and remembering "Oh yeah never mind".


**Twelfth Night, William Shakespeare**

Been reading this to Nathan. In the past I have not loved Shakespeare on the stage -- might have been the hubris of too negative taste of a young man. But reading it, especially out loud, is undeniable.
